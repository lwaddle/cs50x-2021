sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: Best case runtime is very short - on the order of n. Worst
case runtime is slow - on the order of n^2.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: It has the lowest runtime on best and worst case scenarios.

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: Its best and worst case scenarios are just about equal in
runtime.
